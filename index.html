Details

We have plugins for every market!

Our app is the really great for festivals, fairs, theme parks, the zoo and city malls.

Some of our plugins:

  • Friend finder
  • Tent finder
  • Attraction waiting times
  • Artist schedules
  • Walking routes and a lot more!

If you want some special functionality we can always build a custom plugin. If the plugin can be used by another company than the cost will be lower.

Modular system, modular pricing!

One of the things we love to do is designing maps. We can create a special map just for your zoo or city mall. This map will be available for your use outside the application.

The map will be build with tilemill and CartoCSS, so if your house designer would like to help you can always contact us.

Constant improvement, low prices

You know what is really great about our product? Multiple companies are using the same product. This way when company A wants a new custom plugin and company B wants it too, the prices can be cut.

You will buy the product for what is worth for your company, if many customers of you use the app you will pay for the use, if little people use the app you'll have to pay little. This way we want your company to be a sucses and we will make sure the app will be used and promoted best.

Adventure and explore

We really like it to improve your customers experience. Our app is built to quickly find the best spots in your mall or zoo. We have made it so that the user it not spending too much time on their mobile, but is getting good information and a good guide so they can enjoy even more.
