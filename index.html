Overview

Beautiful custom maps!

One of the things we love at Atlast, is designing maps. We will design a colorful map especially for your organisation. We think your app will fit in and improve the overall experience of your customers.

Your organisation may use the map also for other purposes than the app. We don't have restrictive licenses, we like to build maps that give possibilities.

Beautiful custom maps ready for your use.

One system for every device!

What do we need to say more? Whether you carry a smartphone, tablet or just using your computer. Our app will never let you down.

This app runs on everything, always.

Your business is our business!

Truth to be told, you rely on your customers. Whenever you own a shopping mall, a theme park or when you are organizing a big event, we know how to improve the experience of your customers.

Whitelabel

If you wish we will brand the app with your trademark. It is important for us that your app will be a great success. We think that with the right branding, the app will create huge business revenue.

With your feedback all will profit.
